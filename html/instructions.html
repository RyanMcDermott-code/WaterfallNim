Instructions
What is Nim?

Nim is a mathematical game of strategy in which two players take turns removing objects from distinct heaps or piles.




How do you play it?

Players engage in a strategic battle, taking turns skillfully removing matches from rows. After each move, a decisive 'End Turn' button awaits, signaling the shift to the opponent. The ultimate goal? Force your adversary into a position where they're compelled to snatch the last remaining match on the screen. Should you crave a rematch or seek a clean slate, the power to restart the game lies at your fingertips. It's not just a match, it's a thrilling showdown of wits and strategy!
